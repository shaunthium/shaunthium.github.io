Features

Complete privacy

Your data only resides on your local device - no data is ever sent out to anywhere else on the internet.

Track your choices

Use ChoiceTrackr to track the choices you make in your daily life, and see which choices were the best ones for you using the benefit of hindsight.

Personalized recommendations

ChoiceTrackr provides you recommendations based on the choices you've made previously.

Progress tracking

Track your progress in how often you can choose the choices that work best for you!
